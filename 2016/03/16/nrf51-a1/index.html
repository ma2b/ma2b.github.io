Tips:

1.Configure LED-pins as outputs

nrf_gpio_cfg_output();

2.Open LED

nrf_gpio_pin_set();

3.Close LED

nrf_gpio_pin_clear();

4.Toggle LED

nrf_gpio_pin_toggle();

5.Delay with millisecond
